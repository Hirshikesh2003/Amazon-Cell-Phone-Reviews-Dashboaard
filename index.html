About Us

Using techniques of machine learning to analyze and understand customer reviews of cell phones on the Amazon platform.

  • The data is collected, preprocessed to remove irrelevant infromation.
  • Using Sentimental Analysis to determine the overall sentiment.
  • The application of machine learning allows businesses and customers to gain insights into product performance.

NLP Sentimental Analysis is a branch of artificial intelligence that focuses on determining the sentiment or subjective opinion expressed in text data. It involves using machine learning and computational linguistics techniques to automatically analyze and classify the sentiment.
